Forget the Funnel: Unlocking Customer-Led Growth

Are you tired of outdated marketing tactics that treat customers as mere numbers in a funnel? In "Forget the Funnel," you’ll discover a revolutionary approach to growth that places your customers at the heart of your strategy. This book challenges conventional marketing wisdom, showing you how to break free from rigid frameworks and embrace a customer-led mindset that drives real, sustainable growth.

Through practical advice, real-world case studies, and actionable insights, "Forget the Funnel" guides you in understanding your customers deeply, crafting personalized experiences, and building lasting relationships that fuel success. Whether you're a marketer, product leader, or business owner, this book provides the blueprint to reimagine your approach and start growing your business the way it should be—with the customer in mind.

Get ready to forget the funnel and embrace a smarter way to connect, engage, and thrive in today’s competitive landscape!
