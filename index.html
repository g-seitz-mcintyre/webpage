Galileo's Blog

Hi and welcome to my blog. I created this website because I wanted to gain hands-on web development experience. At this point my web development skills are still very rudimentary, but I felt like it's time to get my hands dirty and actually start building something.

Therefore, I'm starting off small by creating a personal blog. I'll be writing about all sorts of things that pique my interest, hoping that by writing about them I'll gain a deeper understanding. Ideally, you as the reader will also be able to learn something.

I'd be happy to hear your thoughts. If you have feedback on the content, or on how to improve the website usability, don't hesitate to reach out via the contact form or connect with me on LinkedIn.

Thank you!
