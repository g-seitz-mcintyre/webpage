Galileo's Blog

Hi and welcome to my blog. I created this website because I wanted to put my web development skills to use. At this point they are still very rudimentary, but I felt like it's time to get my hands dirty and actually start building something.

Therefore, I'm starting off small. Given my job as a Business Analyst at Cheil Germany, I'm often involved in fascinating projects related to Marketing and Digital Transformation. I hope that by writing about these topics I'll gain a better understanding of them, and ideally you as the reader will also be able to learn something.

I'd be happy to hear your thoughts. If you have feedback on both the content of the blog articles, or on how to improve the website usability, don't hesitate to reach out via the contact form or connect with me on LinkedIn.

Thank you!
